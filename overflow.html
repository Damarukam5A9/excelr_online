ChatGPT is a large language model chatbot, developed by OpenAI, that uses natural language processing to engage in human-like conversations and generate text, answering questions, writing different content types, and more. It uses a "Generative Pre-trained Transformer" (GPT) architecture, a type of neural network trained on a massive amount of text data. This training allows it to understand and generate human-like text, including answering questions, writing articles, composing code, and engaging in conversations. Users interact with ChatGPT by typing in prompts or questions, and it then generates a response based on its training and understanding of the input. Capabilities: Question Answering: ChatGPT can answer questions on a wide range of topics. Content Generation: It can generate different types of written content, such as articles, social media posts, essays, emails, and code. Conversational AI: It can engage in human-like conversations, making it useful for customer service, virtual assistants, and other applications. Limitations: ChatGPT is not connected to the internet, and it can occasionally produce incorrect answers. It has limited knowledge of world events after 2021.
